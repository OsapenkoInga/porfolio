PHONE RESERVATIONS FIG accepts reservations up to one month to-the- date in advance for parties of up to six guests. We regret that we cannot accommodate split parties or parties larger than six. A reservationist is available after 10 am Monday- Saturday. So that we may best accommodate all guests, we require a credit card for bookings of 5 or 6 guests. If a reservation for 5 or 6 guests is cancelled with fewer than 48 hours notice, there will be a charge of $50 per person charged to the credit card on file. We encourage you to visit without a reservation! We have seats at our bar during every service available for walk-in dining. 843.805.5900

ONLINE RESERVATIONS Open Table accepts online reservations up to 4 weeks in advance for parties of up to 4 guests. Please note, phone reservations generally become available 2 days in advance of online reservations, so we recommend calling us directly if you have a specific time frame in mind. For booking groups of 5 or 6, we encourage you to call us at 843.805.5900 , as a credit card is required to hold the table. Click below to make a reservation using Open Table. online reservation
